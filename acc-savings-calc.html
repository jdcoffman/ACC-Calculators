Assumptions
ACC cost baselines
$82/credit (in-district) and $283/credit (out-of-district) — these figures include tuition + standard fees. Individual courses may carry additional lab or course fees not reflected here.
4-year school baselines
UT Austin: 2 × $5,429/semester (resident, Liberal Arts, 12+ hrs).

Texas State: $12,220/year (15 hrs/semester basis).

UTSA: $11,448/year (15 hrs/term basis).

Texas A&M: 2 × $6,237.35/semester.

These are resident-rate tuition + fee baselines and do not include housing, meal plans, or transportation.
Scenario definitions
A few classes: 6 credits at ACC. Comparison to a 4-year school is optional and less apples-to-apples.

Associate degree: 60 credits at ACC (2 years full-time). 4-year comparison uses 2 years of the selected school's annual rate.

2+2 pathway: 60 credits at ACC, then 2 more years at the selected 4-year school. Full baseline comparison assumes 4 years at the 4-year school.

Bachelor's at ACC: 120 credits as a full 4-year program at ACC. 4-year comparison uses 4 years at the selected school.
